Is Everything the Best Arrangement?

Philosophy Bilingual
Essay
Publish Date:   2023-11-11
Update Date:   2023-11-11
Word Count:   1.9k
Read Times:   8 Min
Read Count:  

最好的安排，我愿意相信现在的一切都是最好的安排吗，所谓的最好又是按照什么标准来制定的的呢。

The best arrangement. Would I be willing to believe that everything now is the best arrangement? The so-called best is determined by what standard?

假如没有所谓的标准，那最好最坏不就是完全没有意义的事情吗，这就是一种虚无主义

If there is no standard, then best and worst are meaningless, leading to a kind of nihilism.

但是虚无主义在现实生活中是无法存在的，它不是一个答案。

However, nihilism cannot exist in real life; it is not an answer.

说回到底，一切还是要回到自由意志的问题。宇宙是一个统一的终极存在，叫它上帝或者其它任何什么名字都可以，但总之宇宙是一个统一的终极存在。我作为一个个体，是暂时获得了脱离了宇宙这个存在的幻象的一个个体，我的意志决定在本质既是我的作为自觉为孤立的个体做出的，也是宇宙这个存在作为一个整体做出的决定的一个部分。正是因为如此，我的决定从根本上是和宇宙的决定同一的。宇宙是一个完满自足的存在，正因此其完全脱离了欲望和需求的束缚，其所做的决定都完全是行使其完全的自由所做出的。换言之，一个完全自由，不受任何规律束缚，全知全能的存在，其所做出的决定自然是完全旅行了自己的期望的决定，因此是好的。

In the end, everything comes back to the question of free will. The universe is a unified ultimate existence, call it God or any other name, but in any case, the universe is a unified ultimate existence. As an individual, I have a temporary illusion that I am separated from the existence of the universe. But in fact, I make my free decision both as a consciously isolated individual and as a part of the universe. It is precisely because of this that my decision is fundamentally the same as the decision of the universe. The universe is a complete and self-sufficient existence, completely free from the constraints of desires and needs. Its decisions are made exercising its complete freedom. In other words, it is a decision that is completely free, unrestricted by any rules. Universe, as a omniscient and omnipotent existence, makes decisions that naturally align with its own expectations and are therefore good.

当然这不意味着我的决定都本质上被操控因而我只要躺平，毕竟躺平是我的欲望，努力也是我的欲望，期望容易是我的欲望，渴望挑战也是我的欲望，凭什么被一个欲望所操控而躺平，就说是命定的呢。

Of course, this does not mean that my decisions are fundamentally manipulated, so I can just lie down. After all, lying down is my desire, working hard is also my desire; expecting doing easy things is my desire, and surfing challenges is also my desire. Why being controlled by a desire to lie down is the destiny?

为什么对整个宇宙是好的事情，对我来说也是好的呢？因为我作为独立个体的体验是一种幻觉，我在本质上是和整个宇宙为一体的。因此我做出决定，就是宇宙作为整体做出了决定。但这也不是说我的决定可以轻易做出：或许因为宇宙出于体验自我的需要，将自己分割成了表面上无法理解的个体，而让个体在不断的探寻与思考中做出决定，就是宇宙本身决定的一部分。所以作为一个人，该怎么做还是应该怎么做，不能说这就宿命论了，就躺平了。

Why something that is good for the entire universe is also good for me? Because my experience as an independent individual is an illusion; I am fundamentally a part of the entire universe. Therefore, the decisions I make are decisions made by the universe as a whole. But this does not mean that my decisions can be easily made: perhaps because the universe, for the sake of experiencing itself, has divided itself into seemingly incomprehensible individuals and allowed individuals to make decisions through constant exploration and reflection, which is also a part of the universe’s own decision. So as a person, we still need to act as a responsible peron, there is no reason to give up making decisions even if our decisions are parts of the decisions made by universe.

那么，相信这一套宇宙观的好处是什么呢，好处就是，能够相信自己在每时每刻做出的决定，从本质上说都是好的。当然这个好是相对于整个宇宙而言。相信自己所受的境遇，从整体而言就是最好的境遇，从而获得内心的安宁，是相信这套宇宙观的好处。（所以我这个完全是实用主义的立场啊，但是说实话我觉得实用主义的立场确实是最有效的立场）

So, what are the benefits of believing in this world view? The benefit is that you can believe that the decisions you make at every moment are, fundamentally speaking, good. Of course, this goodness is relative to the entire universe. Believing in the circumstances you encounter, from the perspective of the whole, is the best circumstance, thereby gaining inner peace, is the benefit of believing in this world view. (So, my stance is completely pragmatic, but to be honest, I think a pragmatic stance is indeed the most effective stance.)

那么恶行算什么呢，人不应当谴责恶行吗，人不应该为自己的恶行悔过吗，人不应当从善去恶吗。有人说，恶只是达到善的途径，上述的宇宙一体论也表明，局部的恶从整体而言可谓是善。但这不是说恶不应当被谴责、不应当为恶行悔过，因为这些行为，本来就是从恶至善的路径。毕竟，对总体而言的善，对局部而言确实是恶。

Then, how is evil posible? Shouldn’t people condemn evil deeds, repent for their evil deeds, and strive to turn from evil to good? Some say that evil is just a means to achieve good. The aforementioned cosmic monism also indicates that local evil can be considered good from the perspective of the whole. But this does not mean that evil should not be condemned or that one should not repent for evil deeds because these actions, combined with evil, lead to goodness as a whole. And what is good for the whole is indeed evil for the part.

对恶行的审判，首先为社会稳定，二或可起到改造恶人的作用。

To reason to judge evil deeds is to, firstly, maintain social stability. Secondly, it may have the effect of transforming evildoers into better people.

这里稍微有一点跳跃，因为我有点累了不想细想了。总之无论我如何辩护，上述的宇宙一体论思想似乎总是在暗示没有好坏的差别，一切都是宇宙意志的体现，所以人的所有行为都是可以接受的。但是宇宙的意志确实会以各种事情来引导被幻象分割为个体的个人的意志，告诉他们什么是宇宙意志所想要前进的方向。这就是世界的规律，这规律放在自然界就是物理规律，放在生物界就是自然选择等规律，放在人类社会就是社会规律和历史趋势。顺势而为自然就是顺应宇宙意志的前进方向。人类社会的规律是合作共赢。

There’s a slight leap here because I’m a bit tired and don’t want to think too deeply. In any case, no matter how I defend it, the aforementioned cosmic monism thought seems to always imply that there is no difference between good and bad, and everything is an expression of the will of the universe, so all human behavior is acceptable. But the will of the universe does guide individuals who have been divided by illusions in various ways, telling them the direction in which the will of the universe wants to progress. This guidance manifests it self as the law of the world. placed in the natural world as physical laws, in the biological world as laws like natural selection, and in human society as social laws and historical trends. Going with the flow of nature is to go along with the direction of the will of the universe. The law of human society is cooperation and mutual benefit.
